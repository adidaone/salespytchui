We're currently live in 'Private-Beta, What is private-beta?

‘Beta’ is a term used to describe a period that an online service moves through prior to full commercial release. During this time our members will have full use of all of the functionality described above as well as having the opportunity to provide feedback and help us shape the future of the platform.

‘Private’ simply means that we are being selective about who we allow to join at this stage. Sales agents are completely free to join immediately, whereas companies need to apply for a membership and demonstrate that their opportunity is suitable for the caliber of independent sales reps that are joining us.
